Track and update your shows from MyAnimeList.net in a new tab window

Or download and install manually (how to)

Other features

  • Quickly complete shows when you've finished watching
  • See how many episodes are currently aired for your watching shows
  • Completing shows sets the finished date to today on MAL
  • Settings the first episode of a show sets the start date to today on MAL
Or download and install manually (how to)

New Tab: MyAnimeList is a Chrome extension that acts as a startpage and allows you to track and update your shows from MyAnimeList.net quickly and easily in a new tab window.

This application is free of use and in no way affiliated with MyAnimeList.net or AniList.co
